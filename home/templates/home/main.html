About

I'm a sound engineer interested in applying machine learning techniques for scientific research. I am currently part of the Underwater Acoustics Research Group created to develop hardware and software tools to apply passive acoustic monitoring of cetaceans.

This site is meant to showcase a combination of my interests and projects, including bioacoustics, web development, machine learning and some photography gathered during fieldwork. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn.
